I'm designing the unauthenticated user flow for RollAI — a mobile-first web app that analyses BJJ (Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu) training footage and produces a voiced coaching commentary video. The product is aimed at serious BJJ practitioners. The tone is calm, focused, and technical. Think athletic performance tool, not social app.

I want to visually explore four screens before writing any code. Please design them as high-fidelity mobile mockups at 390px wide.
